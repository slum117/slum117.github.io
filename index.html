Background

Our client, Paramveer Singh, has a business that is an event planning organization called Events by Param.
They plan, design and coordinate various events, with a focus on making the client’s life easier
by providing them with the best vendors. Vendors range from décor, photography, venue, catering,
entertainment and many more. Their goal is to save clients time and money by providing step by step coordination.

Scope

We are designing a website with various functions. For example, we will provide customers with the ability to see past
events in the gallery, along with a way to contact event organizers using the UI in the website. A big part of the focus will
be on how the customer interacts with the website so that event organizers will have more information when they meet with
them. So the website will asks for information such as what type of event does the customer plan to have and what time will
be a good time for the customer to meet with an event organizer. There will also be a panel for the customer to include more
information should they desire to do so.
The minimum goals would be to create a fully functioning website that allows customers to find the business and from there,
contact the organizers.

Proposed Solution

We build a website capable of allowing potential customers to contact Events by Param. In addition we also allow those customers
to see what Events by Param can offer them.
